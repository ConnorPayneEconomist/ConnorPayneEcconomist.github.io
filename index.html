Stir Fry Recipe

This is the best recipe you will ever find for a stir fry.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Chicken
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Pak Choi
  • Scallions
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Tenderstem Broccoli
  • Sriracha
  • Naga Chilli

Method

  1. Chop up the vegegables and chicken
  2. Heat the Sesame Oil in a Wok
  3. Add the noodles to a pan of boiling water
  4. Add the choped chicken and cook until brown
  5. Add all the vegetables and cook for a further 2 minutes
  6. Add the Hoisin and Sriracha sause to the Wok. Cook for a further 2 minuets
  7. Drain and add the noodles to the Wok, stir in and serve
